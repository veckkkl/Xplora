# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** May 2026

## Introduction

Xplora (“the app”, “we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the information stored and processed through the application.

This Privacy Policy explains what data Xplora stores, how it is used, and what choices you have regarding your information.

By using Xplora, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

---

## Information We Store

Xplora is designed to help users capture and organize travel experiences, notes, places, and memories.

Depending on how you use the app, the following information may be stored locally on your device:

- Travel notes and written content
- Saved places and location-related information
- Photos attached to notes
- App preferences and settings
- Language selection and display preferences

At this time, Xplora does not require account creation.

---

## Local Storage

Your data is primarily stored locally on your device.

Xplora currently does not provide cloud synchronization or remote account storage. Your notes, places, and preferences remain under your control on the device where the app is installed.

---

## Analytics and Tracking

Xplora does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking technologies at this time.

We do not collect behavioral advertising data or build advertising profiles.

Xplora may generate local in-app statistics based on your saved travel data, such as visited places or travel summaries. These statistics are processed locally on your device and are not shared externally.

If analytics or cloud-based services are introduced in future versions of the app, this Privacy Policy may be updated accordingly.

---

## Third-Party Services

Xplora may use certain Apple-provided system frameworks and services necessary for app functionality, such as:

- UIKit
- Map-related frameworks
- File and photo access permissions
- System sharing features

These services are governed by Apple’s own privacy policies and platform terms.

---

## Permissions

Depending on the features you choose to use, Xplora may request permission to access:

- Photos
- Location services
- Files and documents

These permissions are only used to support the related functionality inside the app.

You can manage permissions at any time in the iOS Settings app.

---

## Data Sharing

Xplora does not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.

Because the app currently stores data locally, your information generally remains on your device unless you explicitly choose to share content using iOS system sharing features.

---

## Data Deletion

You may remove your content at any time directly from the app.

Deleting the app from your device may also remove locally stored data, depending on your iOS backup settings.

---

## Children’s Privacy

Xplora is not directed toward children under the age of 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

---

## Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect improvements, new features, or legal requirements.

Any future updates will be published within the app or through the official project repository.

---

## Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or the app, you may contact the project developer through the official project repository or future support channels.

---

## Summary

Xplora is designed with a simple principle in mind:

Your travel memories belong to you.
